About Me

Hello, I'm MariEinz, a creative explorer at the intersection of audio engineering and computer science.

Armed with a degree in audio engineering, I've spent years crafting captivating soundscapes, mastering the art of music production, and diving deep into audio technology. I've fine-tuned my skills in audio software, honing my craft in sound manipulation and storytelling through music.

But my journey doesn't end there. With a relentless curiosity, I've decided to venture into the world of computer science. This shift represents my dedication to leveraging technology's potential to create innovative audio solutions. My focus is on mastering algorithms, coding languages, and software development to bridge creativity with technology seamlessly.

In my free time, I love experimenting with audio effects, coding software tools, and blending the boundaries of audio and computer science. I see this dual-degree journey as a pathway to driving the future of audio technology, pioneering immersive auditory experiences.
